  • Current Situation
    • Overall Problem
      • Unequal Distribution of Education Resource

        — The distribution of education resource is not fair in different part of America.

        For instance, students in the areas like Alabama cannot receive the same level of education as students in Boston, Massachusetts did.

        We knew that Massachusetts is one of the most wealthy state and Alabama is one of the relatively poor state in the U.S.

        Generally speaking, the commercially poor state cannot offer the same level of education as the wealthier state did.

        It caused inequality in education in different areas.

    Besides the overall problem, students and teachers are faced with detailed problems caused by many issues, for instance, the policies of gun control.
    • Detailed Problems
      • Facing by Students
        • 1: Safety in School
          • The occasional mass shootings happened in American schools, resulting not only the deaths of students, but also the worry of their parents.
          • Students worried about the possibility of gun violence in school and the way to prevent attacks and protect lives of themselves.
          • The Main problems of school safety is that the gun violence cannot be diagnosed in advance. Many mass shooting cases are random crime. Also, the gun control laws are different among areas. It makes mass shooting the initial threat to the school safety.
        • 2: Bullying in School
          • Many students suffered bullying during their secondary studies.
          • A common definition in the research literature is that bullying involves “physical and verbal attacks and harassment directed at a victim(s) by one student or a group of students over an extensive period of time”.
          • There are cases that students do not report after being bullied. They might experience a wide range of problems, including emotional damage, physical injuries.
          • Those problems can deeply impact the students not only during their education stage but also their lives.
          • Cyberbullying, that is, the bullying happened during the use of internet and smartphones, becomes a mainstream bully type these years.
          • Students bullied others online to gain perverted delight.
        • 3: Unified Evaluation Standard
          • The Common Core State Standard is set by the federal government to specify exactly what the students should know during their secondary education stage. It was developed in 2009 to promote educational equality across the country. It requires students to the same standardised test level.
          • It reminds me of the exam-oriented education in China, which deeply stuck students’ creativity and flexibility.
          • It is the similar case in the US. Because of the standardised testing, students and teachers are judged by test scores. It doesn’t have room for teachers and students’ innovation and flexibility with the learning process. That’s what we regularly called Teach-to-the-test approach. It results in the reduction focus of the non-tested subjects like art.
        Besides these problems faced by students, now let’s talk about some problems faced by teachers.
      • Facing by Teachers
        • 1. Decreased Salaries
          • In many states, teacher’s salaries have decreased steadily over the past few years.
          • It can be regarded as the common phenomenon across the whole country.
          • Salary can be regarded as the essential of one’s livelihood. Decrease of salaries might caused much pressures on stuffs like paying the loans. Those pressures might cause the decrease of teaching result of a teacher.
        • 2. Lacking Innovation in standardised exam-oriented education method
          • Teachers’ lacking of innovation is partially caused by the enforcement of standardised testing. America needs teachers who are well-trained to meet the needs of their students who are willing to speak up and be willing to change the current situation. Teachers are on the front lines of educating new generation citizens, however, teachers are being buried in the dilemma of chasing high-score in exams, without their speaking up, change is not possible for the students.
